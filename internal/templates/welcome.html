How to play

Join a room, choose a team, and battle on a grid. Move with WASD or by clicking reachable squares. Shoot enemies to eliminate them; help incapacitated teammates to restore them. Two shots eliminates a player — the killer earns back both shot costs plus a prize.

✦

Support the dev

If you enjoy the game, hit the glowing Donate button in the sidebar. Choose any amount from your session balance — 25%, 50%, or the full pot. Every donation goes directly to the developer's Nano address and is processed on-chain instantly.

⬆

Deposits & withdrawal

Each session gets a unique Nano address. Send XNO to that address to top up your balance — every deposit is recorded with the sender address. When you are done playing, click Withdraw in the sidebar to send your remaining balance back to the address you deposited from. No account needed.

⚠ Start small. Each shot costs 0.0001 XNO, so depositing just 0.0002–0.001 XNO is more than enough to play. Do not send large amounts — this is an experimental game and funds are not insured.
